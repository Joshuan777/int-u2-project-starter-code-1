What is Poverty?

Poverty is people who are struggling to earn money and can't meet the basic needs.

The Impact Poverty has Done

Around the world, there's estimate amount of 700 million people that are poor and are in extreme poverty. That's 8.5% of the global population, surviving on less than $2.15 a day. With the Covid-19 pandemic, economic disruptions, job losses, and rising living causes more families into deeper poverty or go into poverty.
